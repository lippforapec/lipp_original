Lipp

Lipp is a platform to connect startup founders and venture capitalists.

We want to build strong connections between startup founders and VCs. For startup founders, Lipp can help to present your product and company very well. For venture capitalists, we offer a list of amazing startups, which you can find good ones to invest. There are a lot of opportunities of investments in the world. There are also a lot of people who could not take any investment because they are lack of information, because of their genders and prejudices. We think focusing to the product is the first and the most important thing. We are building an environment for the fairness and equality so that anyone who have a good idea can present their products and take investments!
